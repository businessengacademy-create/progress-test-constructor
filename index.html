5How it works — Student workflow

From test creation to results — here's the full journey.

✦
Test is generated
You fill in the form above and click Generate & Download. The AI builds all 40 questions, the reading text, and the listening dialogue. You receive a single .html file.
Teacher
⬆
Published or shared
Upload or publish the file and share the link — or send it directly by email or chat. The file works offline in any browser, no server required.
Teacher
✎
Students take the test
Students open the link, enter their name, and complete all 4 parts: Grammar · Vocabulary · Reading · Listening. The listening section plays directly in the browser using text-to-speech — no headphones required.
Student
📊
Results are shown instantly
On submit, every answer is auto-graded. Correct answers turn green, wrong ones red — with the correct answer shown. Total score and breakdown by part are displayed immediately.
Student
⬇
Student saves & sends PDF
Students click Save Results as PDF — a formatted results sheet is generated locally in the browser (no server needed). They download it and send it to the teacher via email or LMS submission.
